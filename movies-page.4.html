Title: Creed III

Main Actors: Michael B. Jordan, Jonathan Majors, Tessa Thompson, Wood Harris

Age Restriction: PG-13

Show Time: Fri-Sun: 21h:00 - 23h:30

Synposis: In "Creed III," the latest installment in the acclaimed boxing franchise, Adonis Creed, portrayed by Michael B. Jordan, faces his toughest challenge yet, both inside and outside the ring. Having successfully built his boxing career and personal life, Adonis is forced to confront a formidable new rival, Damian Anderson, played by Jonathan Majors, who emerges from a troubled past and seeks to reclaim his place in the sport. As Adonis prepares for this high-stakes bout, he grapples with his own sense of purpose and the pressures of his responsibilities. With intense training sequences, emotional depth, and powerful performances, "Creed III" continues the saga of resilience, family, and the quest for greatness.
