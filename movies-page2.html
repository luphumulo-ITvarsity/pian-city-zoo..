Title:The Karate Kid

Main Actors:Jaden Smith , Jackie Chan , Taraji P.Henson and Wang Wenjie

Age Restriction: PG-9

Show Time: Fri-Sun: 13h:00 - 15h:00

Synposis: In "The Karate Kid," a heartwarming remake of the classic 1984 film, 12-year-old Dre Parker, played by Jaden Smith, moves to China with his mother and struggles to adjust to his new life. He faces bullying at school and finds solace in an unlikely mentor, Mr. Han, portrayed by Jackie Chan. Mr. Han, a skilled martial artist, agrees to teach Dre karate, not just as a form of self-defense, but as a means of personal growth and discipline. As Dre trains, he learns valuable life lessons and gains the confidence to stand up to his bullies, culminating in a dramatic martial arts tournament that tests his newfound skills and spirit.
