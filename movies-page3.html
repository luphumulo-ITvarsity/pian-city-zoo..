Title:Black Panther

Main Actors:Chadwick Boseman, Michael B. Jordan, Lupita Nyong'o, Danai Gurira

Age Restriction: PG-13

Show Time: Fri-Sun: 18h:00 - 20h:00

Synposis: In "Black Panther," the groundbreaking Marvel film, T'Challa, played by Chadwick Boseman, returns to the isolated, technologically advanced African nation of Wakanda to assume the throne as king following his father's death. As he takes on the mantle of the Black Panther, T'Challa must confront challenges both from within his kingdom and from a powerful adversary, Erik Killmonger, portrayed by Michael B. Jordan. The film explores themes of heritage, leadership, and identity as T'Challa navigates political intrigue, battles for his people's future, and seeks to unite his nation. With a powerful story and striking visuals, "Black Panther" delivers a compelling mix of action, drama, and cultural significance.
