Title:Assassins Creed

Main Actors:Sylvester Stallone, Antonio Banderas, Julianne Moore, Bette Midler/p>

Age Restriction: PG-16

Show Time: Thur-Sun: 20h:30 - 23h:00

Synposis: "Assassins" is a high-octane thriller that delves into the world of professional killers and double-crosses. Sylvester Stallone stars as Robert Rath, a seasoned and disillusioned hitman who is contemplating retirement. His plans are disrupted when a ruthless rival, Miguel Bain, played by Antonio Banderas, emerges with a deadly agenda and a personal vendetta. As Rath is drawn back into the game, he must navigate a treacherous web of deception, betrayal, and danger. Julianne Moore and Bette Midler play pivotal roles as individuals who become entangled in the deadly conflict. With a blend of intense action sequences and psychological tension, "Assassins" keeps viewers on the edge of their seats as it explores the dangerous life of professional killers and the price of survival.
