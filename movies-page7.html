Title:The Last Airbender

Main Actors: Noah Ringer, Dev Patel, Nicola Peltz, Jackson Rathbone

Age Restriction: PG

Show Time: Fri-Sun: 13h:00 - 15h:00

Synposis: "The Last Airbender" brings to life the beloved animated series with a visually stunning and epic adventure. The film follows Aang, portrayed by Noah Ringer, a young boy who discovers he is the last of the Airbenders and the fabled Avatar destined to restore balance to a world torn apart by war. As he embarks on his journey, Aang must master the four elements—water, earth, fire, and air—to confront the tyrannical Fire Nation and bring peace to the world. Dev Patel stars as Zuko, a conflicted prince of the Fire Nation determined to capture the Avatar, while Nicola Peltz and Jackson Rathbone play key allies who join Aang on his quest. With its blend of breathtaking action sequences and rich storytelling, "The Last Airbender" captivates audiences with its epic tale of heroism and self-discovery.
